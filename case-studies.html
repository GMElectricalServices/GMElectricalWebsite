Domestic Solar PV & Battery Storage in Penwortham, Preston

Client: Local Homeowner
Service: Solar PV (6kW) with 5kWh Battery Storage
Challenge: Rising energy bills and desire for greater energy independence.

Solution: Designed and installed a bespoke rooftop solar array integrated with a high-capacity battery storage system. This allowed the homeowner to generate their own clean electricity and store excess for use during evenings, significantly reducing reliance on the grid.

Commercial EV Charging Network for Business Park in Bolton

Client: Large Commercial Property Management
Service: Installation of 10 x 22kW EV Chargers with Load Management
Challenge: Providing convenient and scalable EV charging for employees and visitors across multiple office units.

Solution: Implemented a robust network of 22kW fast chargers, strategically placed across the business park's car parks. The system includes smart load management to optimize power distribution and reduce peak demand charges, offering a seamless charging experience for users.

Full Industrial Rewire & LED Lighting Upgrade in Burnley

Client: Manufacturing Facility
Service: Complete Electrical Rewire and Energy-Efficient LED Lighting Conversion
Challenge: Outdated wiring causing frequent trips and high energy consumption from inefficient lighting.

Solution: Executed a full industrial rewire outside of production hours to minimize downtime. Simultaneously, all traditional lighting was replaced with modern, energy-efficient LED fixtures, resulting in enhanced safety, reduced maintenance, and significant electricity bill savings.

School Rooftop Solar PV Project in Blackburn

Client: Primary School
Service: Commercial Solar PV (50kW) Installation
Challenge: Desire to reduce operating costs and teach students about renewable energy.

Solution: Installed a 50kW solar PV system on the school's main building. The project involved careful planning to ensure minimal disruption to school activities. The system now provides a significant portion of the school's electricity, leading to substantial savings and serving as an educational resource.
