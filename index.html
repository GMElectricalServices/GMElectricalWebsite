Welcome to GM Electrical Services

At GM Electrical Services, we are dedicated electrical contractors at the forefront of renewable energy. Our primary expertise lies in delivering high-quality renewable solutions, including the design and installation of efficient Solar PV systems, robust Battery Storage setups for energy independence, and reliable EV Charging infrastructure. Beyond renewables, our certified electricians manage all aspects of general electrical work, ensuring safety and efficiency for homes, businesses, and industrial sites.

We operate from our base in Preston, extending our services to the wider Lancashire area and nationwide across the UK.
