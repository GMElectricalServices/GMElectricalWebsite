Solar PV Solutions

At GM Electrical Services, we specialize in designing and installing high-performance Solar PV (Photovoltaic) systems. By harnessing the sun's energy, you can significantly reduce your electricity bills, lessen your carbon footprint, and contribute to a more sustainable future. We offer tailored solutions for both residential and commercial properties, ensuring optimal energy generation and efficiency.

Our Solar PV Installation Process

We follow a comprehensive and streamlined process to ensure your solar PV installation is smooth, efficient, and delivers optimal results:

1

Desktop Proposal

Initial assessment and detailed proposal tailored to your energy needs.

2

Site Survey

In-depth physical evaluation of your property to confirm feasibility and precise design.

3

DNO (If Applicable)

Handling of District Network Operator application and approval (mandatory for grid connection).

4

Scaffold up

Erection of safe access scaffolding for secure and efficient installation.

5

Installation

Professional mounting of solar panels and electrical connections by certified technicians.

6

Scaffold down

Safe removal of scaffolding upon completion of physical installation.

7

Handover

System commissioning, thorough testing, and comprehensive client training and documentation.

Domestic Solar PV

Transform your home into an energy-independent sanctuary with our bespoke domestic Solar PV installations. We design and install high-performance solar panel systems tailored to your energy consumption, roof type, and aesthetic preferences. Reduce your electricity bills, lower your carbon footprint, and potentially earn from the Smart Export Guarantee (SEG). Our team handles everything from initial consultation and design to installation and commissioning, ensuring a seamless and efficient process.

Why Choose Domestic Solar PV?

  • Significant savings on electricity bills
  • Increased energy independence
  • Reduced carbon footprint
  • Potential income from exporting excess energy
  • Enhance your property's value
  • Long-term reliability with minimal maintenance

Commercial Solar PV

Empower your business with sustainable energy solutions through our commercial Solar PV systems.
