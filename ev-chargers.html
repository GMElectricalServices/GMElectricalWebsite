EV Charger Installations

As electric vehicles become increasingly prevalent, having a reliable and efficient charging solution is essential. GM Electrical Services provides expert installation of EV chargers for homes and businesses across Preston, Lancashire, and the UK. Whether you need a convenient charging point for your daily commute or multiple charging stations for your fleet or customers, we offer a range of solutions from leading manufacturers.

Domestic EV Charger Installation

Enjoy the convenience of charging your electric vehicle from the comfort of your home. We install a variety of domestic EV chargers, including 7kW rapid chargers, ensuring your vehicle is always ready when you are. Our qualified electricians will assess your property's electrical capacity and provide a safe, compliant, and discreet installation.

  • Safe and compliant home charger installation
  • Range of charger models to suit your vehicle and budget
  • Expert advice on charger placement and usage
  • Integration with smart home systems (where compatible)

Commercial EV Charging Solutions

Support your business's transition to electric fleets or provide charging facilities for your employees and customers with our commercial EV charging solutions. We install a range of charger types, from 7kW to 22kW and higher, to meet the demands of various business environments. Enhance your corporate sustainability profile and attract EV drivers to your premises.

  • Scalable charging infrastructure for businesses of all sizes
  • Fast charging options (7kW, 22kW, and beyond)
  • Management systems for access control and billing
  • Guidance on potential government grants and schemes (e.g., OZEV workplace charging scheme)
